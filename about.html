About Me: Chris Wainscott




I started my love for developing web sites around 5 years ago when I was at Indiana Wesleyan University taking some courses in web developing. I was going to start my own web development company after graduation, but found that I needed to know a lot more to be successful in this field. I then had the opportunity to come to the 1150 academy in June 2016 and was able to compliment a lot of my skills. I love working on the front-end design even though I have a lot to learn with photoshop and illustrator. I like to work with dreamweaver and attend a lot of local meetup groups to learn more. I never want to stop learning and this field will definetly help me with that goal. My portfolio is a little different than what the ordinary person might have tried. I wanted to show different aspects of this field and even if I could not finish a project. I wanted to tackle many different things to at least try to figure out how it works. For example, I worked in Unity to create a basketball game and it was a 20 minute tutorial, which after spending 3 hours on it. I choose to stop where I was at and continue with the portfolio.
